Welcome to Trivandrum

Trivandrum, the capital city of the southern Indian state of Kerala, nestled along the Arabian Sea coast, is a historical and cultural hub renowned for its magnificent Padmanabhaswamy Temple and Kovalam Beach. It serves as Kerala's administrative heart, boasting a blend of traditional Kerala culture with a thriving IT sector, represented by the Technopark. Trivandrum's lush landscapes, educational institutions, and vibrant arts scene make it a unique and inviting destination, inviting visitors to explore its rich history, cuisine, and the natural beauty of the region.
